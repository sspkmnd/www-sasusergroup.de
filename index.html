SAS User Group Germany

SAS User Group Germany is a community-driven, non-profit local meetup organisation for everyone interested in the SAS® technology stack – be that pharmaceutical or financial industry, administration, analytics, artificial intelligence & machine learning or just for fun. The meetup is powered by the enthusiasm and curiosity of actual users of SAS software.
